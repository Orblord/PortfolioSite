NASA S.U.I.T.S.

A Hololens based, voice navigated, spatial computing system for astronauts to assist with EVA procedures

About

A.R.S.I.S. is a student project which was done as a submission for the NASA S.U.I.T.S. challenge in 2018 and 2019. The S.U.I.T.S. challenge is an opportunity for students to use augmented reality platforms to create applications which serve as prototypes for a mobile computing system which will eventually be embedded in NASA astronaut helmets used on spacewalks.

I was the principle UX Designer for this project in charge of developing the User Interface for the entire application. In April of 2019 I traveled with a team of students and tested our application in a scenario designed by NASA engineers.

It's Not Me

Combining virtual reality and traditional art to shine a light on the experience of eating disorders and addiction

About

It’s Not Me is a VR experience based on the life and experience of artist Becka Watkins and centers on her lifelong struggle with an eating disorder. Players explore the art and stories Becka produced from young age through adulthood, following her through her struggles and transitions.

The game will ultimately be released for free on all available major platforms and coincide with a national awareness campaign centering around eating disorders.

End Effector

VR game exploring embedded experiences in virtual reality

Story

The year is 3030, far future and technology has evolved to a state unimaginable in our time. Super intelligent robots, realizing their lot in life as human subjugates, have revolted against their meaty overlords. The ensuing war has been brutal, and the humans held the keys to destructive forces embedded deep in the programming of their metal creations. The robots, nearly all but wiped out, retreated deep underground.

You awaken, your programming only recently flashed into your circuitry. You are the new generation of perfect mechanical killing machines, and you need to be trained.

No longer able to trust the programming written by the evil humans, the new class of robots learn through experience. Here, deep underground you must complete the tasks set before you by a mysterious and disembodied overlord. Will you become the next great killer robot?
